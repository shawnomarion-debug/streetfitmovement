Shane Mendes has spent over a decade coaching movement — turning complete beginners into confident movers and athletes into competitors. Multi-certified as a Personal Trainer and Nutrition Coach, he founded STREETFITMOVEMENT around one idea: anyone can transform how they move with the right guidance.

That coaching is backed by a rare competitive pedigree. A winner of Battle of the Year India and a Red Bull BC One finalist, Shane brings world-stage standards to every session — proof the methods work.

His clients range from 8-year-olds discovering movement for the first time to corporate teams unlocking creativity through embodied practice — to competitive athletes preparing for the world stage.
